### ✅ DECIDED: Time Loop
**Original Question:** Commit to time loop or not?

- **Decided:** 2025-10-25
- **Chosen:** NO TIME LOOP (Option 2: Instinct/Trust)
- **Rationale:**
  - Thematically parasitic (distracts from identity/choice themes)
  - Over-engineered complexity
  - Reduces Oak's agency (following script vs. genuine choice)
  - Character-driven trust more emotionally authentic
  - Simpler narrative execution
- **Affected files:**
  - `plot/oak-role-options.md` (marked as decided)
  - `arcs/pokemon-4ever-option.md` (deleted)
  - `arcs/arc1-survival.md` (revised without loop)
  - `core/path-confirmed.md` (confirmed no loop)
