# Arc 2: Hoenn (Years 2-3?)

**Region:** Hoenn
**Duration:** ~6-12 months (flexible, tentative)
**Detail Level:** MODERATE (reasonable to plan, but subject to change)
**Status:** TENTATIVE - This arc is still being shaped

**Read `TONE.md` first** for story approach.

## What Happens (Possible Direction)

Amber travels to Hoenn region. Trapinch's homeland provides opportunity for redemption arc and potential evolution. New Pokemon, different culture, tropical environment. Team Rocket surveillance continues/escalates. Secret may be discovered or nearly revealed. Companions prove loyalty.

**Why Hoenn:**
- Trapinch's homeland (Ground-types, desert areas)
- New region = fresh start attempt
- Different gym system/culture to explore
- Potential: Running from Kanto complications or deliberately traveling

**Major Possible Events:**
- Hoenn arrival and cultural adjustment
- Trapinch homeland exploration/redemption
- Team Rocket escalation (observation → active pursuit)
- Secret complications (companions notice inconsistencies)
- Major confrontation (TR capture attempt)
- Companions prove loyalty despite danger/truth
- Hoenn gym challenges

**Ending State (Tentative):** Team Rocket threat ongoing, found family solidified, more badges, potentially truth shared with companions

**DISCLAIMER:** This is significantly less certain than Arc 1. Story may take different direction based on how Arc 1 concludes.

## Time Frame

Years 2-3 after starting journey (flexible)

## Major Beats

### Setup (Past Catches Up)
- Team Rocket surveillance becomes obvious
- Can't keep running - they know where she is
- Fuji's "death" (fake suicide, actually kept alive by Giovanni)
- Public story: Fuji killed himself in jail
- Amber doesn't know the truth (thinks he's dead)

### Rising Action (Active Pursuit)
- Team Rocket escalates from observation to active pursuit
- Direct attempts to acquire Ditto (rare, perfect clone)
- Companions dragged into danger because of her
- Discovery: she's been tracked since Pallet
- Paranoia: Can't trust random encounters anymore

### Complications (Secrets Under Pressure)
- Companions notice something's off (she's too knowledgeable, too mature)
- Close calls where secret almost revealed
- Sabrina's psychic sense picking up wrongness (if she's present)
- Amber's hyper-vigilance increasing (exhausting to maintain lies)
- Learning more about Fuji's research through Team Rocket encounters

### Midpoint (Truth Discovered or Nearly Revealed)
**Option A:** Companions discover truth
- Through Sabrina's psychic sense, or
- Team Rocket confrontation reveals it, or
- Amber's cover blown in crisis moment

**Option B:** Nearly revealed but saved
- Close call creates even more tension
- Now actively lying to friends (worse than omission)

**Either way:** Test of relationships - do they stay or leave?

### Crisis (Major Confrontation)
- Team Rocket makes serious move (capture attempt, ambush, siege)
- Can't run anymore - have to fight
- Uses everything learned in Arc 1 (survival skills, partnerships, strategy)
- Companions prove loyalty (fight alongside her despite danger/truth)
- Oak intervention? (depending on what's decided)

### Climax (Consequences and Choices)
- Team Rocket threat temporarily neutralized (not defeated, just delayed)
- Relationships changed by truth (closer or complicated, not destroyed)
- Amber forced to confront: Can't keep running forever
- Decision: Stop hiding so much, or dig deeper?

### Resolution (Moving Forward)
- Found family solidified (chose to stay despite everything)
- Team Rocket still out there (will return in Arc 3)
- Amber accepting: Secret causes problems but friends stayed anyway
- Sets up Arc 3: Mewtwo encounter, final confrontation

## Character Arc

**Start:** Hiding origins, afraid of discovery, hyper-independent
**Journey:** Secrets cause complications, relationships tested under pressure, can't run forever
**End:** Accepting that truth is complicated but friends stayed anyway

## Challenge Type

**Escalating danger + secret under pressure**
- Team Rocket actively hunting her/Ditto
- Physical danger increases (ambushes, confrontations)
- Emotional: Exhausting to maintain lies
- Social: What if companions find out and leave?
- Paranoia: Can't trust anyone new

## Growth Demonstrated

- Can't handle everything alone (Arc 1 lesson tested harder)
- Trusting companions even when scared
- Accepting help despite vulnerability
- Fighting back instead of just running
- Understanding: Relationships survive truth

## Story Elements Driving This Arc

**Team Rocket escalation:**
- Passive tracking → active pursuit
- Grunts → serious operatives
- Observation → capture attempts
- Creates immediate danger and tension

**Secrets causing problems:**
- Harder to explain knowledge/maturity
- Companions noticing inconsistencies
- Close calls during TR confrontations
- Lying to friends feels worse than to strangers

**Found family tested:**
- Will they stay when they learn truth?
- Fighting alongside her despite danger
- Choosing loyalty over safety
- Bonds prove stronger than origin
