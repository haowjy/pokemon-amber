## Growth Over Time

**Realistic progression:**
- Age 10 (Arc 1): Survival, barely scraping by
- Age 11-12 (Arc 2-3): Competent but still learning
- Age 13-15 (Arc 4?): Strong trainer, can return to Kanto confidently
- Age 16-17+ (Arc 5-6): Elite-level capability, final confrontations

**NOT instant power:**
- Growth takes years
- Setbacks and failures along the way
- Economic struggles ease gradually
- Team expands slowly as she can afford it
- Becomes elite through accumulated experience, not destiny
