# Future Arc: Kitakami

**Status:** Far future. Seed only. Details TBD.

## What It Is

Kitakami is a small, completely isolated village (population ~55) in a mountainous region surrounded by dense dragon-type Pokemon territory. Nobody in the outside world knows it exists — only rumors. The dragon gauntlet (Gible dens, Jangmo-o packs, Kommo-o deeper in) has kept it inaccessible for generations.

**Amber is the one who gets through and discovers it for the world.**

## Why Nobody Knows

The routes in are Dragon country:
- Gible/Gabite/Garchomp in the mountain passes (Crystal Pool, Oni Mountain)
- Jangmo-o/Hakamo-o/Kommo-o in the wilds and Paradise Barrens
- Fully evolved dragons deeper in

Most travelers turn back without knowing there's anything on the other side.

## The Village

- Two settlements: Mossui Town (pop. ~17) and Kitakami Hall (pop. ~38)
- Deeply traditional; annual Festival of Masks
- The village has been closed off long enough to develop xenophobia

## The Ogerpon Mythology (The Buried Truth)

The official village history: three heroic Pokemon (the Loyal Three — Okidogi, Munkidori, Fezandipiti) protected the village from an evil ogre. Worshipped for generations.

**The actual history:** Ogerpon and a man arrived as outsiders, were rejected for being different. A compassionate mask-maker gave them four masks. A Pokemon named Pecharunt sent the "Loyal Three" to steal the masks. The man was injured protecting one. Ogerpon drove the thieves away, killing three. Villagers misread the aftermath and built a mythology around the wrong side.

The truth was passed down only through the mask-maker's descendants. The rest of the village has been wrong for 300 years.

## Why This Resonates With Amber

- Amber knows the games — she knows the Ogerpon truth before the village does
- Themes of misremembered history and the outsider misjudged map directly onto her own story
- She's an outsider herself (isekai'd, clone) who has been misread her whole existence

## The Oak Connection

Oak pushed into the Kitakami outskirts as a young trainer, was driven back by a Kommo-o, and never found out what was there. He has been telling this story for forty years. It has no ending.

**Payoff:** After Amber discovers Kitakami, she tells Oak what was on the other side. He spent forty years wondering.
