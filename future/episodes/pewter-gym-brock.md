# Pewter Gym & Brock Reunion

**Episode: Emotional payoff from Flint encounter**

## Episode Context

**Amber's Journey So Far:**
- Route 1 solo survival
- Flint + Trapinch rescue
- Viridian (license exam, official Trapinch catch)
- Traveling north to Pewter

**Walking:**
- Viridian to Pewter (through Viridian Forest route)
- Quest boards along the way for food money
- Pokemon Center stays when affordable

---

## Arrival at Pewter

### First Impressions:

**Pewter City:**
- Rock/mining town aesthetic
- Mt. Moon visible in distance
- Pokemon Center near gym (Amber's first stop)
- City is modest, working-class feel

**Amber's Goal:**
- Challenge Pewter Gym (Badge 1 attempt)
- Brock is Gym Leader (Flint has returned but passed leadership to Brock)
- Doesn't know if Flint mentioned her

---

## The Gym Encounter

### At The Door:

**Amber arrives at gym:**
- Standard gym building (rock garden outside)
- Younger Harrison siblings visible (some playing with Geodude)
- One of them: "Are you here to challenge Brock?"
- Amber: "Yes."

**Inside:**
- Rock-type gym aesthetic (boulders, training equipment)
- Brock appears (15, squinting eyes, already responsible)

### The Recognition:

**Brock sees her:**
- Purple hair, Ditto at side, young (10 years old)
- His eyes widen slightly

**Brock:** "You're... the girl with the Ditto. From Viridian Forest."

**Amber:** (surprised he knows) "How did you...?"

**Brock:** "My dad came home a few weeks ago. He told us about you."

**Pause - emotional weight**

**Brock:** (genuine, slightly thick voice) "I don't know what you said to him out there, but... thank you. For bringing him home."

**Amber:** (uncomfortable with gratitude) "I... didn't do much. We just talked."

**Brock:** "It was enough."

---

## The Gym Challenge

### Pre-Battle Conversation:

**Brock:** "So you're here for a badge challenge?"

**Amber:** "Yeah. If that's okay."

**Brock:** (professional) "Of course. Do you have your Trainer License?"

**Amber:** (shows license - freshly earned)

**Brock:** (nods) "Alright. This will be a two-Pokemon battle. I'll scale to your level - you have two Pokemon registered, so this is your first gym, right?"

**Amber:** "Yes."

**Brock:** "No problem. I'll use Pokemon appropriate for a first badge challenge. Let me get set up."

**Amber's internal:**
- He's being fair, not giving her a free pass
- Grateful for that (doesn't want charity)
- Nervous (first official gym battle)

### The Battle:

**Brock's Team (First Badge Level):**
- **Geodude** (primary)
- **Onix** (if needed - likely holds back)

**Amber's Team:**
- **Ditto** (versatile)
- **Trapinch** (defensive, slow)

**Battle Structure:**

**Round 1: Ditto vs. Geodude**
- Ditto Transforms into Geodude (mirrors strategy)
- Rock vs. Rock (even matchup)
- Amber uses adult tactical knowledge
- Brock impressed by her strategy
- Ditto wins (barely, exhaustion)

**Round 2: Trapinch vs. Onix** (if Brock sends it out)
- Onix is MUCH stronger (Rock/Ground)
- Trapinch defensive (Dig to avoid, Sand Tomb for control)
- Can't win straight combat
- Amber focuses on: survive, control, tire out
- **Either:** Trapinch loses (respectable showing) OR wins through persistence
  - **Recommended:** Trapinch wins (barely, through defensive tactics)
  - Shows: strategy + persistence > raw power
  - Brock impressed: "You know how to work with what you have."

### Post-Battle:

**If Amber wins:**
- Brock: "That was really well-fought. You've got good tactical sense."
- Awards Boulder Badge
- "You earned this. Congratulations."

**If Amber loses one round:**
- Brock: "You fought smart. Your Ditto's versatility is impressive, and your Trapinch held out longer than most first-timers."
- Awards Boulder Badge anyway (first gym is teaching moment)
- "You showed enough skill. The badge is yours."

**Amber's relief:**
- **Badge 1 earned** (officially on the journey)
- Brock was fair but supportive
- Not charity, genuine recognition

---

## The Conversation After

### In Gym Office/Lobby:

**Brock:** "So, where are you headed next?"

**Amber:** "Not sure yet. Maybe Cerulean? Or back south."

**Brock:** "You traveling alone?"

**Amber:** "Yeah. Just me and my Pokemon."

**Brock:** (recognizes the pattern - like his dad's story) "That's tough. Especially at ten."

**Amber:** (shrugs) "I'm managing."

**Brock:** "I can tell." (pause) "Look, if you're ever passing through Pewter again and need a place to stay... we have space. Just ask."

**Amber:** (touched, unsure how to respond) "...Thanks."

### About Flint:

**Amber:** "How is he? Your dad?"

**Brock:** (small smile) "Better. He's helping with the younger kids now. Still figuring things out, but... he's here. That's what matters."

**Amber:** "I'm glad."

**Brock:** "He told me about the Trapinch rescue. Said you handled yourself well even against Team Rocket."

**Amber:** "We both did. I couldn't have managed alone."

**Brock:** "Sounds like you're learning that lesson faster than most. Accepting help is hard."

**Amber:** (quiet) "Yeah."

### The Offer:

**Brock:** "Hey, if you need supplies or advice before leaving Pewter... come by anytime. Dad mentioned you're doing this without family support."

**Amber:** "I don't want charity."

**Brock:** "Not charity. Just... community. Trainers help each other. That's how it works."

**Amber:** (considers) "...Okay. Thanks."

**Brock:** "Take care of yourself out there. And your Trapinch - make sure it's getting enough calcium supplements for that shell."

**Amber:** (surprised he noticed) "I will."
