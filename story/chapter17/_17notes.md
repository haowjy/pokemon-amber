# Chapter 17 - Notes

- Story: ./17chapter.md

## Seeded Beats (For Future Payoff)

### Oak / Sam Connection
Oak's behavior in this chapter is informed by having met Amber as young Sam during the 4Ever events — which haven't happened yet from Amber's perspective. He carries a forty-year-old memory of her.

- *"There was something in his eyes --- recognition? Certainty?"* — not suspicion about her being unusual; he knows her face
- Age question ("How old are you?") — Oak calculating how long until her journey, and therefore when the 4Ever events happen for her. She deflects; he still doesn't know exactly when.
- The Kitakami story and "I met people I wouldn't trade for anything" — he is not talking about the village. He is talking about Amber. Gary has heard this story a hundred times without knowing what it's actually about.
- Oak lets her get away with deflections and evasions because he already knows her. Forty years of knowing.

### The Kitakami Story
The adventure Oak starts telling is about pushing into the outskirts of a dragon-infested mountain range at age 17, chasing rumors of a lost village nobody had ever found. He made it as far as **Wistful Fields** (outer edge — Jangmo-o packs, Gible dens) before being driven back. His Charmeleon evolved into Charizard during this push.

He never found out what was on the other side. The story has no ending — that's why Gary is tired of it.

**Future payoff:** Amber is the one who gets through and discovers Kitakami. She tells Oak what was there.
