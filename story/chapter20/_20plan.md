# Chapter 20 Plan: The Cave (Working Title)

**POV:** Amber (1st person)
**Timeline:** Day 2 of journey (after departure in Ch 18, Route 1 Day 1 in Ch 19)
**Location:** Route 1 -> off-route forest -> cave system near Viridian

---

## Pre-Cave Setup (may be late Ch 19 or early Ch 20)

### Caterpie Catch (Day 1)
- Amber's first real catch on Route 1. Simple, classic, not flashy.
- She's riding the high of her first trainer moment.

### Teddiursa Encounter
- Amber encounters an aggressive Teddiursa off or near the route.
- It attacks her on sight --- hostile, not playful.
- She switches from Caterpie to Ditto. Ditto wins.
- Amber catches Teddiursa with HP lowered --- **catches in a single try**, which she notes feels weirdly lucky (two catches in a row on the first ball).
- Releases Teddiursa from the ball. It immediately turns hostile again, tries to attack her. She returns it fast.

### Amber Puts It Together
- Thinks about it. Realizes: if there's a Teddiursa, there should be a mother nearby.
- Waits on high alert for a few minutes. No Ursaring comes.
- A pit forms in her stomach. The mother isn't coming. The mother is *missing*.

### The Deal
- Releases Teddiursa again, this time with a prepared speech.
- **"Your mother is missing, right? I'll help find her."**
- Teddiursa stops. Considers the offer.
- Ditto and Teddiursa communicate while Amber watches, nodding along, half-understanding the gestures.
- Teddiursa points in a direction, gestures taller than Amber, holds up 2 fingers.
- **Amber translates:** "2 humans, both taller than me, went that direction?"
- Teddiursa nods aggressively and starts tracking in that direction.

---

## Off-Route Journey

- Teddiursa leads them off the route, past the other side of the path, deeper into the forest.
- Amber gets nervous but doesn't say anything --- she just made a promise.
- Half a day of nonstop tracking. Sun is starting to set.
- They luckily don't encounter any wild Pokemon on the way.
- They reach a cave entrance. Amber gets a *feeling* --- something strange about this cave, she doesn't know what.
- **Amber insists they prepare before going in.** Rest, eat, stock up. Teddiursa enjoys the berries she brought. Amber prepares revives and potions.
- They enter the cave as evening falls.

---

## The Cave Fight

### What They Find
- Deep enough into the cave, they stumble on a fight already in progress.
- **Charcadet vs. Houndoom + Persian** --- two TR grunts' Pokemon, 2v1.
- The fight is surprisingly even. Amber quickly realizes why: Houndoom keeps using fire moves, which have no effect on Charcadet (fire type) --- may even power it up. The grunts don't seem to realize this.
- But the 2v1 and Houndoom's non-fire moves still make it close. The pair is slowly winning.

### Charcadet Context (not known to Amber yet)
- Charcadet has been in this cave for centuries, loyal to its dead master (a Paldean noble, long dead --- skeleton deeper in the cave).
- A psychic stone recently appeared nearby (ultra wormhole/dungeon event), which attracted TR's sensors.
- TR sent 2 grunts to investigate the energy reading. They found Charcadet guarding its master's resting place and attacked.

### Teddiursa Charges
- Before Amber can plan a sneak attack or assess the situation, Teddiursa immediately rushes out to attack the 2 TR trainers.
- The grunts notice. They redirect Persian to deal with Teddiursa.

### Amber's Instinct --- THE MOMENT
- In the heat of adrenaline, Amber's body moves before her brain does.
- She jumps in and tackles Teddiursa out of Persian's attack path.
- She gets hurt --- Persian's claws rake her back. Blood.
- **This is not calculated. Not strategic. Pure instinct --- protecting something smaller than her.**
- **This is what Charcadet sees.** A human threw herself in front of a hit for a Pokemon. A knight recognizes bravery and loyalty. This is the seed.

### Ditto Transforms
- Ditto, seeing its trainer get hurt, acts immediately.
- It transforms into **Arcanine** --- from the Oak household. Ditto remembers this form.
- Rushes in to defend Amber.
- **Amber is shocked and confused.** There is no Arcanine here. Where did--- what? She doesn't understand how Ditto pulled this form. Mystery for her to process later.

### The Tide Turns
- Fight splits: Charcadet vs. Houndoom, Ditto-Arcanine vs. Persian.
- Charcadet is winning 1v1 now that fire moves aren't landing.
- Ditto-Arcanine is stronger than Persian.
- TR grunts realize they're losing. Start to retreat.

### Ursaring
- As a last resort / distraction, the TR grunts release **Ursaring**.
- Ursaring immediately starts rampaging --- she's been captured, she's furious.
- The cave starts shaking. Ursaring's thrashing causes structural collapse.
- **Teddiursa sees his mother.** He shouts. She's RIGHT THERE.
- But it's too late --- Ursaring's rampage is bringing the cave down. Before Ursaring realizes her own cub is in danger, the TR grunts have already recalled her and are running.
- **The cave collapses around Amber, Charcadet, Ditto, and Teddiursa.**
- Teddiursa saw his mother and couldn't reach her. The promise stays open. The debt deepens.

---

## The Escape

- Cave entrance is collapsed. TR is gone. Ursaring is gone.
- Amber is hurt --- Persian clawed her back badly. There's blood.
- Charcadet knows this cave. It's been living here for centuries.
- **Charcadet leads them to another way out** --- a path it knows through the deeper cave system.
- Amber is weak from the injury. She can barely walk.
- Ditto (still as Arcanine) carries her on its back as Charcadet guides them out.
- They emerge somewhere and make their way to **Viridian City Pokemon Center**.

### What Leaves the Cave
- **The psychic stone** --- Charcadet carries it out. It's been guarding this cave and its master's resting place; the stone appeared near the grave. It's not leaving it behind for a stranger. Amber doesn't know what it is or why Charcadet is holding it. The stone transfers to Amber later --- whenever Charcadet trusts her enough to let her carry it. That's a relationship milestone, not a Day 2 event. (This eventually becomes the Mega Stone ore / Mewtwonite Y origin.)
- **Charcadet** --- it follows them out. Not captured yet. Not bonded yet. But it saw what she did, and its master's grave is now buried under rubble. There's nothing left to guard.

---

## Aftermath / Chapter End

- Amber arrives at Viridian Pokemon Center injured, riding Ditto-Arcanine, with a Teddiursa and a Charcadet nobody in Kanto has ever documented.
- She needs medical attention. Her Pokemon need healing.
- Teddiursa is grief-stricken --- saw his mother and lost her again.
- Charcadet is carrying a strange stone it took from the cave. Amber doesn't know what it is.

### Charcadet's Choice (at the Pokemon Center)
- Charcadet hasn't been captured yet. It followed them out but hasn't pledged anything.
- Briefly, Amber catches Charcadet and Teddiursa in quiet communication --- Charcadet speaking to Teddy in gestures she can't fully follow. Something is being decided between them. Teddy nods.
- Then Charcadet turns to Amber. **Gets down on one knee.** A knight's acceptance. It's choosing her.
- Amber catches it. Simple. Almost ceremonial.

### Ditto-Arcanine --- Figured Out
- Amber asks Oak (by phone, or thinks it through herself with what she knows). The answer lands: Ditto lived in Oak's household. It saw Arcanine. It remembered.
- The mystery isn't resolved with a dramatic reveal --- it's resolved with a quiet realization. Ditto has a life before Amber. It carries memories she doesn't know about.
- **Next training session:** Ditto transforms into Arcanine again, deliberately this time. Remembering, not reacting. A small, tender beat about Ditto's depth.

### The Stone (long thread)
- Charcadet keeps the stone. Amber doesn't ask for it. It's not hers.
- **Handoff happens much later --- right before Arc 4 (4Ever).** Charcadet trusts her enough by then to let her carry it. For readers paying attention, this is a quiet red flag: the moment Amber takes the stone is the moment before she loses Charcadet. (See `future/arcs/saga-overview.md` Arc 4.)

## Thematic Beats

**Faith (knightly virtue):** Amber made a promise to Teddiursa and followed through. Every step off-route, every nervous moment she didn't turn back --- she kept her word. The reader sees this even though Charcadet doesn't.

**Bravery / Loyalty (what Charcadet sees):** Amber threw herself in front of Persian to protect Teddiursa. Not calculated. Instinct. A knight recognizes this --- protecting something weaker than you at cost to yourself.

**Charcadet's grief:** Its master is centuries dead. The grave is now buried. There's nothing to guard. Amber didn't replace the old master --- she just showed up and did something worth noticing. The bond is a seed, not a finished thing.

**Teddiursa's open wound:** He saw his mother. She was right there. TR took her again. The promise is still open.

**Ditto's depth:** It pulled a form Amber didn't know it had. Oak's Arcanine. This is a thread about what Ditto actually is and what it remembers from before Amber.
